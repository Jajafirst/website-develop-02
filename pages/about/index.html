About Me

Hi, I’m First — a creative technologist, designer, and developer who turns ideas into real, functional, beautiful things.

Currently studying Computer Science, I build across code, visuals, and systems. Whether I’m crafting a web app that connects gamers for ranked sessions, designing a smooth UX for a cognitive skill test, or debugging a 2D shark shooter game in Java — I live at the intersection of logic and creativity.

I’m also the founder of FirstForge Studio, where I explore themes like stillness, chaos, and identity through digital art, visual drops, and interactive projects. My style blends mood-driven storytelling with functional design. Every pixel, every line of code — it’s all intentional.

What drives me:

  • Building systems that actually work
  • Making things people want to touch, feel, or play with
  • Creating from emotion — but delivering with structure
  • Turning messy ideas into experiences people remember

I’m fluent in HTML/CSS/JS, Java, Python, and Google Apps Script. I love automation, visual design, and building things that feel effortless to use.
